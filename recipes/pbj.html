Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A basic peanut butter and jelly sandwich

A childhood staple, the venerable PB and J has satified many a hungry child.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 T peanut butter
  • 1 T grape jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Smash the slices together.
  4. Enjoy!
